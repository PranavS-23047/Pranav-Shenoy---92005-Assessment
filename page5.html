If you want to speak your mind here are a few things you can do to get more involved :

UNICEF - Donation WWF New Zealand - Donation Only One - Petition Change.org - Petition Conservation Volunteers New Zealand - Volunteer

The United Nations Children's Fund, or UNICEF is a worldwide agency dedicated to providing humanitarian and developmental aid to children worldwide. UNICEF's mission is to protect the rights of every child, ensuring they have access to basic needs like healthcare, education, and protection from violence and exploitation.
WWF-New Zealand is part of the WWF International Network, the world's largest independent conservation organisation. WWF's mission is to stop the degradation of the planet's natural environment and to build a future in which people live in harmony with nature.
Only One is a community-led organization dedicated to restoring ocean health and tackling the climate crisis. They connect people with actions and campaigns aimed at protecting the environment, particularly the oceans, and promoting climate action. The organization utilizes storytelling, collective action, and crowdfunding to mobilize people and support solutions for ocean and climate challenges.
Change.org is a platform that enables individuals to start and promote online petitions to drive social and political change. It acts as a space for people to voice their concerns, raise awareness, and potentially influence decision-makers on various issues. Essentially, it's a tool for citizen-led activism, allowing anyone to create and share petitions on a wide range of topics.
Conservation Volunteers New Zealand (CVNZ) is a non-profit organization that mobilizes volunteers to participate in various conservation projects across New Zealand. They focus on activities like planting native trees, weeding, pest control, and restoring wild places. CVNZ aims to empower communities to become kaitiaki (guardians) of nature and enhance the health of New Zealand's environment.

Above are overviews of each organization. Each paragraph contains the names of each programme and what they are striving to achieve. The headings above are interactable links that can be used to navigate to the page. Each link takes you to either a donation, petition or volunteering link and has been noted as well.
