Biodiversity as a whole

Biodiversity is a serious issue which is immensely affecting the greater part of New Zealand. From the shores of the oceans to the peaks of mountains we are constantly losing what makes us truly special. Our trees are dying, our animals are going extinct but what we should keep is our will to fight. Our will to fight for our land is going to be the guiding light back to restoring Aotearoa back to how it was then. Pristine and serene.
