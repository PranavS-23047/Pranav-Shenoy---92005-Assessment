What is the main problem?

There are many factors to consider when the term biodiversity loss is being used so there is no real main problem. In New Zealand specifically, we have faced a big on-land problem which was the introduction of invasive species, predators and herbivores. This has led to a decline in numbers for many indigenous species as well as extinction which includes plants and animals.
